How Food Rescue Hub Works

Our simple process makes sharing surplus food with those in need quick and efficient

Simple Steps to Share Food

Follow these four easy steps to donate your surplus food and make a difference

1
📸

Take a Photo

Snap a quick pic of the food you're sharing. Clear photos help recipients know exactly what's available.

Tip: Make sure the photo is well-lit and shows the food clearly.
2
🏷️

Select Food Type

Choose if your food is Veg, Non-Veg, or Animal-friendly. This makes it easy for the right recipient to find it.

Tip: Be accurate with food type to ensure it reaches appropriate recipients.
3
📝

Add Details

Enter quantity, pickup time, and location. The more details you provide, the easier it is for recipients to plan.

Tip: Be specific about pickup windows to coordinate smoothly with recipients.
4
↗️

Post & Connect Instantly

Your listing goes live immediately. We'll notify nearby users - you just hand it over with a smile.

Tip: Be ready for quick responses, especially during peak hours.
