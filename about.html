About Food Rescue Hub

Learn about our mission to reduce food waste and nourish communities

Our Story

Food Rescue Hub was founded in 2023 with a simple but powerful vision: to create a world where no edible food goes to waste while people go hungry. Our platform bridges the gap between food donors and receivers, making food redistribution efficient, transparent, and impactful.

What started as a small community initiative has grown into a nationwide movement. We've witnessed firsthand how technology can transform the way we address food insecurity and environmental sustainability. By connecting surplus food from businesses and households with local charities and individuals in need, we're building stronger, more resilient communities.

Our Mission

To reduce food waste and fight hunger by connecting surplus food from businesses and households with local charities and individuals in need through an innovative, location-based matching platform.

We believe that access to nutritious food is a fundamental human right. By leveraging technology and community partnerships, we're creating systemic change that addresses both food waste and food insecurity simultaneously.
