How It Works

Our simple three-step process makes food rescue efficient and effective.

1

Register & List

Donors register and post details of available surplus food including quantity, location, and pickup time.

2

Match & Notify

Our system matches the food with nearby receivers and sends instant notifications.

3

Schedule & Collect

The first receiver to respond can claim the donation and schedule a pickup.
